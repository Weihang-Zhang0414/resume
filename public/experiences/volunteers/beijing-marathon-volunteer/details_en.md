# Beijing Marathon Volunteer

**Volunteer** | November 2024 - November 2024

**TL;DR:** Provided volunteer services at the starting zone of the Beijing Marathon in Tiananmen Square, coordinating crowd flow, guiding international runners, and maintaining perimeter safety.

## 🌟 Background & Motivation
To ensure crowd safety and operational organization in the high-density starting zones of the Beijing Marathon, facilitating fluid grid entries for thousands of competitors.

## 🤝 Volunteer Work Details
* **Starting Zone Grid Coordination:** Guided arriving runners to designated wave sections in Tiananmen Square. Handled race morning inquiries regarding bag check trucks, water stations, and timing zones.
* **Perimeter Logistics Support:** Assisted the organizing committee in resetting perimeter fences post-start and sorting site waste, ensuring prompt restoration of the city center plaza.

## ✨ Reflections & Social Impact
* Supported the orderly start of thousands of marathon runners without crowding incidents or safety hazards.
* Recognized for civic hospitality, earning the Beijing Marathon Organizing Committee's Volunteer Service Certificate.
