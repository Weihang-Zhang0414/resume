# Youth Cultural Exchange Program

**Youth Ambassador** | August 2026

**TL;DR:** Served as a student representative under a prominent US cultural exchange initiative, home-staying with American host families, surveying East/West coast universities and social infrastructure, and delivering interactive Chinese culture workshops.

## 🎯 Objectives
To foster mutual understanding between Chinese and American youth. Aimed to conduct field studies regarding higher education methodologies, local public safety networks, and NGO workflows while representing Chinese cultural traditions to community members.

## 💻 Skills & Keywords
Cross-Cultural Communication, Social Research, English Presentation, Teamwork, Non-Profit Service Studies

## 🛠️ Core Contributions
* **American Homestay Integration:** Lived with local host families in local neighborhoods, joining family events and discussions on domestic family patterns, cultural differences, and civic engagement.
* **Academic & Social Systems Research:** Conducted academic tours of UCLA, Princeton, and UPenn (Wharton School). Visited local public service structures (fire departments, police departments) and civic NGOs (e.g., Food Banks) to study municipal management and non-profit logistics.
* **Chinese Cultural Outreach:** Conceived and delivered interactive workshops showcasing traditional elements, including Hanfu, Chinese paper-cutting, and calligraphic brush arts, to host networks and peers.

## 🏆 Key Results & Quantified Impact
* Completed an immersive exchange tour, generating a comparative analysis report regarding US community-based volunteering programs.
* Promoted interpersonal diplomacy, hosting interactive cultural showcases for over 30 international participants.
* Solidified advanced conversational fluency and cross-border project coordination capabilities.
