# Intelligent Badminton Robot

**Team Leader** | April 2024 - April 2024

**TL;DR:** Completed the full 3D CAD modeling and conceptual system design of an autonomous badminton-playing robot, outlining sensory fusion pipelines and trajectory prediction algorithms during my freshman year.

## 🎯 Project Objectives
To design an autonomous robot capable of mobile navigation, real-time 3D flight trajectory tracking of a shuttlecock, and active racket-swing interception. Although physical prototyping was constrained by resource scale, the project successfully delivered a mature mechanical design and hardware control architecture.

## 💻 Tech Stack
SolidWorks 3D Modeling, Mechanical Mechanism Design, Hardware Selection (LiDAR / Industrial Cameras / Servo Motors), Kinematic Calculation, Trajectory Prediction Formulation

## 🛠️ Core Contributions
* **3D Assembly & Mechanism Design:** Created detailed SolidWorks 3D assembly models of the robot, including a Mecanum-wheeled omnidirectional platform, a high-frequency slider-crank racket swinging mechanism, and a scissor-lift height adjustment system.
* **Sensory System Engineering:** Structured the hardware integration topology, selecting high-frame-rate stereo cameras for 3D shuttlecock visual mapping and 2D LiDAR for simultaneous localization and mapping (SLAM) on the court.
* **Control Algorithm Formulation:** Drafted mathematical frameworks for flight trajectory prediction (quadratic drag models) and formulated mobile path alignment strategies under strict real-time response constraints.

## 🏆 Key Results & Quantified Impact
* Delivered a complete CAD package detailing assemblies, bills of materials (BOM), and mechanical linkages.
* Authored a comprehensive technical feasibility report defining response loop timing constraints (sensing-to-actuation target within 300ms).
* Conducted independent design defense during the undergraduate research competition, demonstrating strong multidisciplinary system analysis skills.
