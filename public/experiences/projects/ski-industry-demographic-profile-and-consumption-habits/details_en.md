# Ski Industry Demographic Profile and Consumption Habits

**Team Member** | October 2024 - June 2025

**TL;DR:** Conducted market research on the skiing industry using a combination of survey design and Python web scrapers, extracting social media reviews to perform NLP sentiment analysis and keyword word cloud generation.

## 🎯 Project Objectives
To construct a multidimensional consumer profile for winter sports in China. By combining structured survey data with unstructured social media commentary, the project aims to uncover consumer motivations, spending structures, preferences, and service pain points, offering strategic marketing advice for ski resorts and retail brands.

## 💻 Tech Stack
Python, Web Scraping (Requests / BeautifulSoup / Scrapy), Natural Language Processing (NLP / Jieba), Word Cloud Generation, Data Analysis (Pandas / NumPy / Matplotlib), Survey Design

## 🛠️ Core Contributions
* **Questionnaire Design & Data Analysis:** Formulated a structured market questionnaire covering skiing habits, expenditures, branding preferences, and travel factors. Analyzed returned surveys via Pandas cross-tabulation to map consumer spending segments.
* **Social Media Scraper Development:** Developed custom Python crawlers to extract tens of thousands of ski-related posts and user reviews from mainstream platforms (e.g., Xiaohongshu, Weibo).
* **Text Mining & Word Cloud Generation:** Applied the `Jieba` library for Chinese word segmentation and text cleaning (removing stop words). Used the `WordCloud` library to map high-frequency keywords, visually highlighting top concerns such as "snow quality," "instructor pricing," and "transportation."

## 🏆 Key Results & Quantified Impact
* Successfully collected high-quality survey samples and crawled/processed over 10,000 text data points.
* Generated a detailed consumer demographic report identifying three key user segments ("tourist beginners," "enthusiasts," and "family recreation groups") along with distinct purchasing drivers.
* The analysis provided valuable business development frameworks praised in market research competitions.
